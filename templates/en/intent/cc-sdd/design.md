## Design Hints

Bullet the perspectives that are easy to overlook when cc-sdd generates the design:

- Responsibility boundaries (what this scope owns / does not own)
- Dependency direction
- Handling of side effects
- Phased migration policy / rollback perspective
- Risks
- Options considered but not adopted
